Travel Recs

Where to visit if you end up where I was this Summer!

New York City

Rilakkuma Taikyaki NYC

This Japanese Ice Cream Parlor offers various flavors of soft serve, such as Ube, Matcha, Black Tea, Strawberry, and Mango. Instead of a traditional ice cream cone, it's served in a 'taiyaki,' a traditional Japanese fish-shaped cake. The warm cake compliments the cold ice cream, making for a delicious and exciting treat!



Seattle

Starbucks Reserve Roastery

This isn't your typical Starbucks. Starbucks Reserves offer both freshly ground and imported coffee, exceeding the quality of its standard branches. You can purchase coffee tasting flights, enjoy siphon-brewed coffee, or try one of their innovative tea combinations. A must-try for the caffeine fiend in your life.



Denver

RiNo Rock Climbing Gym

Colorado is known for its climbing scene, and it's easy to join in on the fun! As they say, if you can climb a ladder, you can climb a wall. RiNo is a bouldering gym, meaning you can go on your own and scale courses without the help of a belayer. There's offerings for all skill levels, ranging from quick warmups to mind-bending puzzles. If you've every wanted to try climbing, this would be a great place to start!
